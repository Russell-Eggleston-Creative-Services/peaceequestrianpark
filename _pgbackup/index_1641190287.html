About
Peace Equestrian Park

Peace Equestrian Park is located 10 minutes from Fort St. John, BC on the edge of the scenic Peace River valley. 

Specializing in breeding and raising high performance show horses and ponies. Horses and ponies from our farm have found their forever homes all over Canada and the US. 

Horses For Sale

High quality bloodlines meets well tempered champions.

Our horses and ponies are bred and raised with the upmost care and compassion. Visit our YouTube Channel to view our current selection for sale.

Riding Lessons

Confidence on and off the saddle.

Riding lessons available for the very beginner to the seasoned equestrian. Learn how to know your horse in the saddle and ride in harmony with them. You will also learn how to care for your horse off the saddle and develop a relationship with them. Dressage, jumping and general equestrian available.
